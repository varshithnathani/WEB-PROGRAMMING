Fitter
Overview of the ITI Fitter Program
Our ITI Fitter program is crafted to provide students with a solid foundation in the principles of fitting and machining, combining theoretical instruction with hands-on experience. Participants will learn essential skills such as interpreting engineering drawings, working with various tools, and understanding assembly techniques. Under the guidance of experienced instructors, students will engage in practical projects that enhance their technical proficiency and problem-solving abilities. Graduates will emerge ready to seize diverse career opportunities in industries like manufacturing, construction, and automotive. Join us to kickstart your career in fitting and unlock your potential in this vital trade!
Electrical
Overview of the ITI Electrical Program
Our ITI Electrical program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills essential for success in the electrical industry. Participants will delve into key subjects such as electrical circuits, wiring techniques, and safety standards, all under the guidance of experienced instructors. The program emphasizes hands-on training, allowing students to work on real-world projects that enhance their problem-solving abilities and technical expertise. Graduates will be well-equipped to pursue diverse career opportunities in sectors like construction, manufacturing, and renewable energy. Join us to embark on a rewarding journey in electrical engineering and unlock your potential!
Mechanic
Overview of the ITI Mechanic Program
Our ITI Mechanic program offers a comprehensive education designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed for a successful career in automotive and mechanical industries. The curriculum covers essential topics such as engine repair, maintenance techniques, and diagnostic procedures, combining theoretical learning with extensive hands-on training. With guidance from experienced instructors, students will work on real-world projects that enhance their technical skills and problem-solving abilities. Graduates will be well-prepared for a variety of roles in sectors like automotive, manufacturing, and repair services. Join us to start your journey in mechanics and unlock your potential in this dynamic field!
Eligibility Criteria
  • Candidates must have passed the class 10th examination from a recognised educational board.
  • Candidates must be 14 years old or older.
